Who is JK150?

My name is JK150, or "John Kyle" for short. I am a professional artist, musical composer, SM64 Machinimist, and mostly YouTube who constantly makes content all the time. Most of you people are wondering why I made that name behind it. In April 2018, I named the channel and started creating content like Baldi's Basics or something. Now, I'll be making wacky SM64 Machinima content to entertain and make the audience laugh.

What is SixtyTunes?

SixtyTunes is the name itself created by himself "JK150", as the music channel. Behind it's name, SixtyTunes is based on the consoles name "Nintendo 64", instead of the company name to avoid plagiarizing.

Since when did you launch your first YouTube channel and how did you find its name?

I first created the channel in 2016 and started creating TTS content filled with action and tense. However, in 2018, the channel was closed due to many issues and the content was changed. And thus, the name "JK150" was born. However, the content is not the same and it shut down due to personal issues. In 2019, I launched a channel called "JKMB" but now the content is filled with SM64 Machinimas. However, in December due to COPPA's law, I nearly lost my content again. And relaunch in 2020, return to making my content once more. A few months later, my name was revived to "JK150". And that’s the end of how I show my whole history behind all of it.
